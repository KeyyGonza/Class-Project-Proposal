Application Overview

The K-Pop-Playlist-Pals is a music generator website that allows the user to press a button based on their mood and a random song will appear.

Some moods include, happy, love, sad, and chill. Allowing the user to explore new artist and songs.

When pressing the song link, it will lead to Youtube in order to listen to the song.

The Quiz allows users to answer 6 questions and in the end, they will be given a list of K-pop groups that match what they answered
